5. Troubleshooting Steps

If you're still getting "Missing required OAuth data" error:
  1. Check Backend Logs: Look at your PHP error logs to see what data is actually received
  2. Verify Endpoint: Make sure your backend endpoint is at /api/auth/google
  3. Check CORS: Ensure your backend allows requests from http://localhost:3000
  4. Data Validation: Verify your backend is checking for google_id, email, and full_name
